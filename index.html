About

Research at the intersection of probability, analysis and computation.

I am a PhD candidate at Imperial College London, supervised by Prof. Dan Crisan. My research focuses on approximating solutions of stochastic partial differential equations (SPDEs) using interacting particle systems.

I am affiliated with the Mathematics for our Future Climate Centre for Doctoral Training (MFC CDT), a joint programme with the University of Reading and the University of Southampton.
