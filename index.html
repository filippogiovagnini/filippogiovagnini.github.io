I am a PhD candidate at Imperial College London, under the supervision of Prof. Dan Crisan. My research focuses on the approximation of solutions of Stochastic Partial Differential Equations (SPDEs) by interacting particle systems.

I am also affiliated with the Mathematics for our Future Climate Center for Doctoral Training (MFC CDT), which is a joint program with the University of Reading and the University of Southampton.
